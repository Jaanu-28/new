Tracer

Overwatch Character

Tracer is a fictional character from the video game Overwatch. Her real name is Lena Oxton. She is known for her speed and time-jumping abilities.

Abilities

  • Blink (short teleport)
  • Recall (go back in time)
  • Pulse Pistols
  • Pulse Bomb (ultimate)

Famous quote: "Cheers, love! The cavalry's here!"
